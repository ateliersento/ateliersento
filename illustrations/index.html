FRESCOES

We created 2 big illustrations and 3 yôkai characters that were printed as frescoes on the walls of Tsundoku comic book store in Marseille, France. It's a project in collaboration with Mathieu Bablet who created the interior design of the store.

Ukiyo-e japanese print

We imagined this illustration for the Japanese workshop Miyakodori who created the print using traditional Japanese techniques of Ukiyo-e: woodcarving and washi paper. This was a project currated by the artist Shinji Tsuchimochi.
