About

SANGO is a free adventure game inspired by The Coral Cave world. It contains 2 mini stories created during 3 hours workshops in march 2017. It was written and designed by Atelier Sentô and drawn with colored pencils by children at the Châtenay school and the public library of Chassieu.

Developer: Atelier Sentô and children from the school Le Châtenay and the public library of Chassieu
Languages: English, French, Japanese
Genre: point-and-click
Software: Unity
Release date: December 2017
Platforms: PC / Mac / Linux

THE VILLAGE OF CRABS

A little turtle helps a girl to find her brother who lives in the sea.

Screenshots


DOWNLOAD HD screenshots and logos


This game was created during a workshop with 10-year-old children from the elementary school of Le Châtenay, in collaboration with the library of Chassieu on Friday, March 24 2017. The children drew the characters and backgrounds using colored pencils; their drawings were then scanned and included in the game, which took shape before their eyes. At the end of the 3-hour workshop the first version of the game could be played.

The Song of the Whale

One day, Mochi the cat discover a huge whale was floating in the sky. Her song is so sad that Mochi decides to find a way to help her.
