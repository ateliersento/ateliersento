About

Yūrei Station is a short and free point&click adventure game created during a workshop in collaboration with art college students from La Joliverie, Nantes, France. We made the programming, story and music and the students worked on the graphics, backgrounds and characters. Everything is hand drawn with pencils and painted with watercolors.

Developer: Atelier Sentô and students from La Joliverie (Pôle des Arts Graphiques)
Genre: point-and-click
Software: Unity
Release date: April 2017
Platforms: PC / Mac

Play the game on itch.io

Story

A young Japanese girl receive strange messages on her mobile phone since her boyfriend disappeared. One day, the messages guide her to a strange desterted unnamed village in the deap countryside, at the edge of the woods...

workshop at La Joliverie

Yurei Sation was created during a workshop with around 20 students from the French graphic art schoolLa Joliverie in march 2017. The students drew all the graphics in only 2 days. Each student choosed a part of the background (tree, house, mountain...) and drew a sketch on paper. Then they used a light box to report the drawings on a good paper with a 8b pencil. The drawings are painted with watercolor. They putted a blue tint on most of the elements because the scene is happening early in the morning. At the end, They scan all the pictures and cut them on a software to put them in the game.



UNITY

We used Unity and the plugin Adventure Creator. Each 2D item is integrated in a 3D area, like a diorama. The camera is set behind the main character and follows his moving to create an immersion.



Character design

The main characters are a young Japanese girl and her boyfriend nowadays, living in the countryside. The girl's walking animation is hand drawn on paper, frame by frame.
