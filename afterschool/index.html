About

Every evening, as she walks back from school, a Japanese girl stops at the window of the boy she loves in secret. But he only has eyes for his books and doesn't notice her.
The girl dreams of the day she will find the strength to talk to him.

After School is a free interactive short story. It was developed in 2 days, during a masterclass with Game Art students at ECV art school in Bordeaux, France.

Developer: Atelier Sentô and students from ECV Bordeaux
Languages: English, French
Genre: adventure, romance, point and click
Software: Unity
Release date: 2016
Platforms: PC / Mac / Linux / Android
