Festival of Shadows

Coming in March 2023

    MORE INFO

In a Japanese village, the inhabitants are entrusted to assist the "shadows" of those who died tragically to find peace. Naoko, a dreamy young woman, is given a year to save the soul of a mysterious man. As the years goes by, Naoko finds herself teetering between the worlds of the living and the dead. Could she be risking her own life to help someone who has already lost his?

ONIBI

ENGLISH VERSION
JAPANESE VERSION

Graphic novel
128 pages
Cecile and Olivier travel to a remote corner of Japan. Along the way, they purchase an old camera that has the ability to capture images of Japan's invisible spirit world. With their magical camera, they explore the countryside and meet people who tell them about the forgotten ghosts, ghouls and demons. These Yokai, or supernatural beings, are sometimes kind, sometimes mischievous, and sometimes downright dangerous!
