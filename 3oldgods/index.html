About

3 Old Gods is a short movie made in collaboration with I-media college of Niigata.
The story takes place in contemporary Japan. 3 gods walk down the mountain and meet in town for having a drink and talk about the old days.

Directed by Atelier Sentô
Scenario: Josh Tierney
Cast: Kôsuke Baba, Wataru Tsumoto, Yasuo Gondaira and Aida Hisako
Staff: I-media college students
Release date: 2012
Time: 14:23
Language: Japanese
Subtitles: English, French



3 Old Gods is a short film about three supposed, forgotten Japanese gods — or perhaps they're just crazy old men.
